Frequently Asked Questions

What does a Solar EPC company do?

A Solar EPC company handles the full process, from design and procurement of materials based on customer choice to installation, testing, commissioning, and ensuring government compliance, making it a one-stop solution for solar energy projects.

Why should I choose an EPC company over individual contractors?

Choosing an EPC company ensures expertise, quality work, and faster completion, as they handle everything under one roof, avoiding the delays and coordination issues common with individual contractors.

What factors should I consider when choosing a Solar EPC company in Bangalore?

Key factors include the company's expertise, industry experience, the quality of their work, and their ability to manage complex projects seamlessly while ensuring government compliance.

How long does it take for a Solar EPC project to complete?

A Solar EPC project can take 1 to 2 months, depending on the kilowatt capacity and specific project requirements.

Are Solar EPC services in Bangalore eligible for government subsidies or incentives?

Yes, if the EPC company is registered as an MNRE (Ministry of New and Renewable Energy) partner and empaneled, they can help you avail of government subsidies and incentives for solar installations.

What makes Apollo Power Systems a top Solar EPC company in Bangalore?

Apollo Power Systems excels with its expert team, strong experience in the industry, and a prestigious client portfolio including Prestige, Raheja, Brigade, Google, VRL, and Total Energies.
